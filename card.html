Cat

The average cat sleeps 16-18 hours per day.

  • Persian cat: long-haired breed of cat characterized by its round face and short muzzle
  • Ragdoll: The Ragdoll is a cat breed with blue eyes and a distinct colorpoint coat
  • Siamese cat: The Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian cat.
  • British Shorthair: is the pedigreed version of the traditional British domestic cat

The domestic cat is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal. They are often called house cats when kept as indoor pets or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them from other felids and felines.
